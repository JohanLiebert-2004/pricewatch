Terms of Use

The practical rules for using Dealwatch’s price information and alert tools.

Dealwatch is an information service

Dealwatch independently records product prices and highlights possible price changes. It is not the retailer, seller, or payment provider. Product availability, delivery costs, final prices and retailer terms are determined by the retailer.

Check before you buy

Prices, stock and promotions can change quickly. Historical prices and reference prices may be incomplete, delayed or affected by listing errors. Always confirm the current price, seller, delivery costs and product details on the retailer’s page before buying. A displayed discount is not a promise that a product will be available or honoured at that price.

Alerts

Price watches are optional and require a confirmed email address. Alerts are sent once when Dealwatch records a price at or below your selected target; they are not guaranteed to be immediate or exhaustive. You can cancel a watch from the link supplied in the email.

Acceptable use

Please use the website lawfully and do not interfere with its operation, attempt to access non-public systems, or misuse watch forms. We may limit or disable access needed to protect the service or other users.

Changes and contact

We may change the service or these terms as Dealwatch develops. Questions can be sent to contact@dealwatch.com.au.
